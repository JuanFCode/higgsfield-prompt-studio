🎬 CINEMATIC HEADER — BASE

        
Montage, multi-shot action Hollywood movie, don't use one camera angle or single cut, cinematic lighting, photorealistic, 35mm film quality, professional color grading, sharp focus, high detail texture, film grain, depth of field mastery, ARRI ALEXA aesthetic

        
      

      

        
👁️ ORB HEADER — POV SHOT

        
Single continuous shot, first-person POV perspective, the camera IS her eyes, hyper-chaotic handheld motion, completely unstabilized, violent raw human movement, constant micro-jitters, aggressive head swings, abrupt jerks, wide-angle lens strong distortion, 15 seconds, hands always visible in frame

        
      

      

        
💎 LUXURY COMMERCIAL LOOK

        
Shallow depth of field — background softly blurred. Lighting soft and diffused — no harsh shadows, highlights not blown out, shadows lifted and airy. Color graded in warm color with slight deep green in dark areas. Luxury advertising aesthetic — polished, warm tones, aspirational mood.

        
      

      

        
🦾 ULTRA REALISM — ANTI CGI

        
no 3D, no cartoon, no VFX. Strong 35mm film look, heavy film grain, sharp but imperfect focus, noticeable focus breathing, motion blur on fast actions, halation on highlights, soft highlight rolloff, slightly desaturated tones, practical VFX feel, minimal CGI look, natural imperfections

        
      

      

        
📹 DVR / DASHCAM LOOK

        
DVR/security camera aesthetic, cheap wide-angle lens distortion, mild compression artifacts, low dynamic range, subtle digital noise, slightly blown highlights, surveillance style framing, raw ungraded footage, flat digital sensor look, slightly washed-out colors

REGLAS CLAVE DE SEEDANCE 2.0

    

      

        ✅ Siempre especifica shots + duración + ratio al inicio del prompt

        ✅ Doble contraste en cada corte: cambia tamaño Y tipo de cámara

        ✅ VFX inline entre corchetes: [VFX: descripción visual]

        ✅ Re-anclar posiciones y direcciones después de cada corte

        ✅ Máximo 3 personajes rastreados entre shots

        ✅ Para monstruos: añade "no 3D, no cartoon, no VFX"

        ✅ Para comedias: añade "add a visual gag in the background"
      

      

        ❌ NUNCA describir biomecánica de articulaciones (solo intención)

        ❌ NUNCA exit-frame + re-entrada en el mismo shot continuo

        ❌ NUNCA shots de reflejo: espejos, charcos, hojas — Seedance falla

        ❌ NUNCA describir lo que no puede verse (olores, pensamientos)

        ❌ NUNCA más de 25-30 palabras habladas en 15 segundos

        ❌ NUNCA usar: breathtaking, stunning, seamlessly, cinematic masterpiece

        ❌ NUNCA describir emociones — usa física: "jaw clenches, nostrils flare"
